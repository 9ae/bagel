## Page 1

### Panel 1

EXT. FOREST - NIGHT 

The moon is full tonight, in a dense forest is small clearing. Two camping lanterns rest on the ground.

DIANE, mid 30s Chinese woman, wearing black windbreaker digs furiously with a shovel.

ELANOR, late 20s Korean woman, in a cream dress with blood stains on the front skirt, sits on a double bass case. Next to her is a sapling tree about 4 ft high in a pot.1 1
